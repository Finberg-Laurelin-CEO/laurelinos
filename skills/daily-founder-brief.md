# Daily Founder Brief

## Goal

Produce a concise daily operating brief for a founder.

## Inputs

- recent notes;
- calendar events;
- open commitments;
- customer/investor updates;
- engineering/project updates;
- previous brief.

## Output

1. What changed.
2. What needs attention.
3. Open loops.
4. Upcoming meetings.
5. Recommended next actions.
6. Source citations.

## Safety

Do not send messages, schedule meetings, or write to external tools without approval.
